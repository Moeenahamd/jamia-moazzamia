JAMIA MOAZZAMIA ( KHAWAJA MUHAMMAD HUSSAIN CAMPUS)

In this Islamic institution, approximately 400 students are currently enrolled in various courses. These courses include Hifz-e-Quran (memorization of the Quran), Tajweed (the rules of Quranic recitation), Dars-e-Nizami (an eight-year Islamic scholarship program), Matriculation (secondary school education), Intermediate (higher secondary education), ADP/B.A. (Associate Degree Program/Bachelor of Arts) spanning two years, BS English (Bachelor of Science in English) with a four-year duration, BS Islamiyat/Arabic, and Computer Courses. This institution provides a comprehensive educational platform for students to study and excel in Islamic studies, language proficiency, general education, and computer skills.
