KHANQAH E MOAZZAMIA

Khanqah e Moazzamia is an institution consisting of Boys & Girls Campuses, along with AL Moazzam Girls College and AL Moazzam University. The organization operates Free Homeo Despensaries, providing healthcare services, and Dar ul Iftah, offering religious guidance. Libraries are available for students and researchers, and the institution conducts Tarbiyati Conferences and Seminars. With a commitment to service, Jamia Moazzamia operates 24/7 to serve humanity. Additionally, the AL Hameed Water Filtration Service ensures clean drinking water for communities. Overall, Jamia Moazzamia encompasses educational, healthcare, religious, and welfare initiatives, promoting holistic development and well-being.

KHAWAJA MUHAMMAD HUSSAIN CAMPUS

This Islamic institution is home to approximately 400 students who are engaged in a variety of courses. These include Hifz-e-Quran, where students memorize the entire Quran, and Tajweed, which focuses on the proper recitation and pronunciation of the Quranic verses. The institution also offers an extensive eight-year program called Dars-e-Nizami, covering subjects such as Islamic theology, jurisprudence, Quranic exegesis, Hadith studies, and Arabic language. For those pursuing formal education, matriculation and intermediate programs are available. Additionally, the institution provides ADP/B.A (2 years) programs, BS English (4 years), BS Islamiyat/Arabic, and computer courses. This comprehensive range of courses ensures that students receive a well-rounded education with a focus on Islamic studies, language proficiency, and technical skills.
