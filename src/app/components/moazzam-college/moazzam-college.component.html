AL MOAZZAM GIRLS COLLAGE

Al Moazzam College offers a wide range of academic programs to cater to diverse interests and career paths. These programs include F.A. (Faculty of Arts), Education, Citizenship, Psychology, Computer Science, Health and Physical Science, F.Sc. (Faculty of Science), ICS (Intermediate in Computer Science), I.COM (Intermediate in Commerce), ADP (Associate Degree in Education), Journalism, Political Science, B.Sc. (Bachelor of Science), M.A. (Master of Arts), English, Islamiyat (Islamic Studies), and Education. This comprehensive selection allows students to pursue their desired fields of study and acquire knowledge in various disciplines.
