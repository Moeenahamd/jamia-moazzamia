KHANQAH E MOAZZAMIA

Khanqah e Moazzamia is an institution consisting of Boys & Girls Campuses, along with AL Moazzam Girls College and AL Moazzam University. The organization operates Free Homeo Despensaries, providing healthcare services, and Dar ul Iftah, offering religious guidance. Libraries are available for students and researchers, and the institution conducts Tarbiyati Conferences and Seminars. With a commitment to service, Jamia Moazzamia operates 24/7 to serve humanity. Additionally, the AL Hameed Water Filtration Service ensures clean drinking water for communities. Overall, Jamia Moazzamia encompasses educational, healthcare, religious, and welfare initiatives, promoting holistic development and well-being.
