LIBRARY OF JAMIA MOAZZAMIA

In the four marla Hall roundabout, there is a collection of approximately 7000 books covering various subjects. The collection includes books on Aqaaeed (Islamic creed), Tafseer ul Quran (interpretation of the Quran), Hadith and Seerah (prophetic traditions and biography), Fiqh and Usool e Fiqh (Islamic jurisprudence and principles), Philosophy, History, Isma o Rejaal (the study of narrators in Hadith), Arabic Literature, Persian Literature, Urdu Literature, Qalmi Nuskhaajaat (manuscripts and calligraphy), and Literature on Sufism. These books provide a diverse range of knowledge and insights into these respective fields.
